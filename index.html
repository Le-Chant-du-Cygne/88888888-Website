88888888

88888888 est un dispositif ludique dans lequel le joueur manipule des filtres de couleur afin de rendre visible et compréhensible l'architecture de l'espace virtuel dans lequel il est immergé.

Credits

88888888 is a Le chant du cygne collective's project, created by Simon Chauvin, Romain Enselme and Gaël Bourhis.

This project was initiated during the Jam Mozilla Lumière in Paris, and developed in Gamelier's game lab and Openlab's fab lab.

It was exhibited during the Take The Blows Event @Badaboum.
